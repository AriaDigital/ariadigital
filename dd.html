Logo and Branding

Drunken Dungeons is a small Twitch and YouTube channel focused on casual gaming with a "drinking rules" twist. Consisting of four core members and featuring a multitude of friends; the channel founder wanted the logo to focus on the style of content instead of the most common cast. The most important social site was the Twitch channel page, followed by matching YouTube and Twitter icons and banners to make Drunken Dungeons content swiftly recognizable. Later, t-shirt designs were requested for a friendly gathering of Drunken Dungeons members and friends. I managed the printing process in addition to designs for the front and back of the t-shirts.
